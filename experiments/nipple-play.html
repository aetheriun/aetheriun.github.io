Nipple Play (2024)

~ ITCH.IO PAGE ~
~ GAMEPLAY FOOTAGE ~

Made during Game-A-Week Melbourne 2024, for the theme of 'Weird Input',
'Nipple Play' is an alt-controller game made using Unity, Arduino and two potentiometers.
Twist the potentiometers, etch-a-sketch style, and bring pleasure to your partner.

Nipple Play has been exhibited at Sabby Gallery, as part of the Game-A-Week 2024 Play Party.
