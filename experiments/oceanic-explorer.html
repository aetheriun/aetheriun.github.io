Oceanic Explorer (2020)

~ ITCH.IO PAGE ~


'Oceanic Explorer' is a piece of digital literature describing a never-ending nautical adventure.
Made during the Toolkits : Digital Storytelling 2020 developmental writing program, it uses Tracery.js to generate fragments of narrative text, and embedds them in an animated oceanscape coded in p5.js
