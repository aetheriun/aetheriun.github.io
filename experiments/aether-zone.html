The Aether Zone (2022)

~ VIEW ONLINE ~

'The Aether Zone' is a Geocities inspired website hand coded as a container for a collection of experimental game prototypes and digital art pieces, made as responses to weekly themes.
Each object is contained on its own page, lovingly stylized to reflect either the content of the prototype, or the theme it was resoinding to, or both!
