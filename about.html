Eamonn Harte is a writer and game developer from Melbourne, Australia.

They are passionate about storytelling and narrative in interactive forms, as well as experimenting playfully with a variety of digital tools.

They are currently completing their Masters of Animation, Games and Interactivity at RMIT University; and have exhibited games and interactive work at ACMI, Sabby Gallery, and the Melbourne Queer Games Festival.
