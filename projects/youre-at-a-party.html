You're At A Party (2024)

'You're At A Party' is an interactive fiction game made in Unity, with the narrative scripting language Ink, that explores the complexities of queer relationships.
Unfolding over one night, at a houseparty in a suburban sharehouse, the player comes face-to-face with their ex and his new partner, and has to navigate the tense, awkward situation that unfolds after that.
