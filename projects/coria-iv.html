Coria IV (2024)

'Coria IV' is a retro-futurism inspired sci-fi walking simulator game made in Unity.
Featuring an episodic branching narrative; players are able to freely explore a remote interstellar moon-base, and use a conversational interface to communicate with an NPC character.
Choices made in the conversation affect the outcome of the narrative, and the environment of the game changes over time to reflect the state of the relationship between player and NPC.
